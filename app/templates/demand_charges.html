About Demand Charges

Demand charges are fees based on your peak power usage (measured in kW) during specific time periods. These are separate from energy charges (which are based on total kWh consumed).

Configure your utility's demand charge periods below to help Tesla optimize when to charge and discharge your battery to minimize demand charges.
