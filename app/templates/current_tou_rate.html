How It Works

Current Tariff: The tariff shown above is what's currently programmed in your Tesla Powerwall. This is what your Powerwall uses to optimize battery charging and discharging.

Saving Profiles: You can save snapshots of your current tariff at any time. This is useful:

  • Before switching to Amber Electric - save your original utility tariff
  • Before testing custom TOU schedules
  • To keep different seasonal rate configurations
  • As a backup before making manual changes

Restoring Profiles: Click "Restore" on any saved profile to upload it back to your Powerwall. The Powerwall will immediately start using the restored tariff for optimization.

Note: Automatic Amber syncing (if enabled) will overwrite the tariff every 5 minutes. Disable automatic syncing on the dashboard if you want to use a saved profile permanently.
